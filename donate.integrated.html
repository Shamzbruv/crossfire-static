Frequently Asked Questions

Is online giving secure?

Yes, all transactions are processed through secure, encrypted payment gateways that protect your financial information.

Can I set up recurring giving?

Yes, both Givelify and PayPal support recurring donations. You can set the frequency and amount during the donation process.

Will I receive a receipt for tax purposes?

Yes, you will receive an email receipt immediately after your donation. We also provide annual giving statements for tax purposes.

What if I need help with my donation?

Contact us at crossfireworshipcenter@gmail.com and we'll be happy to assist you.

Give via Zelle

Follow these steps to send your donation using Zelle:

1
Open your banking app or Zelle app
2
Select "Send Money with Zelle"
3
Enter the church's Zelle email address:
crossfireworshipcenter@gmail.com
4
Enter the amount you wish to donate
5
In the memo/note field, specify: [Purpose]
6
Review and send your donation

Note: Zelle is available in most banking apps. If you don't see Zelle in your app, you can download the standalone Zelle app from your device's app store.
